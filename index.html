Logos InkPen 1.0.2

Dedicated to FreeHand Lovers Everywhere

A massive update with native FreeHand file import. FH3 through FH11 and FreeHand MX, opened directly without any intermediate formats. v1.0.2 available for download.

PDF and SVG parsing has been greatly improved. Perfect for Xcode UI, SpriteKit sprites, web graphics, and Linux icons. It can even open your PDF receipts and W-2 forms. Supports Apple Pencil via Luna Display or Sidecar.

Built from the ground up on SwiftUI, Metal & CoreGraphics for macOS 14, 15 & 26. Engineered for blazing-fast rendering with GPU-accelerated paths, real-time previews, and instant zoom at any scale. Logos InkPen is designed to be the fastest vector art & design app on the planet.
